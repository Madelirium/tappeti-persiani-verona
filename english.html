About

Since 1991, the authenticity of Persian rugs in Verona

Shahmansouri Persian Rugs brings to Verona the timeless charm of Persian rugs, shaped by an art handed down through generations. Selected materials and skilled craftsmanship give life to unique pieces made to last and tell a story.

The collection brings together antique rugs of historical value, contemporary creations with refined design and classic expressions of Persian tradition. Every detail, from knot to weave, helps turn a home into a place of harmony, warmth and elegance.

Useful tip

Rotating a rug by 180 degrees every 6 months helps distribute sunlight and foot traffic more evenly, reducing visible wear in the most used areas.

Frequently asked questions

Useful answers before entrusting a rug

How much does rug cleaning cost?

The cost depends on size, materials, level of dirt and the type of treatment required. In the shop we can suggest the most suitable treatment and provide a quote.

When is it worth restoring an antique rug?

It is worth intervening when edges start to give way, fringes are worn, or there are small holes and fragile areas. Early action prevents more serious damage.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Yes, for Verona and nearby areas we can evaluate pickup and delivery, especially for large or delicate rugs.
